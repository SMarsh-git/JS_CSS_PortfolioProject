2021

Site Administrator - PMP Recruitment

In early 2021 I was brought on to PMP recruitment as a Site Administrator under the Kick Start scheme. I worked onsite in a large supermarket chain's warehouse, I was placed there to help with the PMP's onsite office for their continual recruitment for the warehouse.

My duties included the admin side of the recruitment aswell as some of the hands on activity. I would call all applicants as soon as they had applied were I would prescreen them to ensure they were upto the sites requirements and regulations. If a candidate was suitable I would arrange for them to come to the site for our weekly site tour, I would also ensure that candidates had the approriate working boots and high visibilty jackets. If they did not I would arrange their provision for the site tour. After all candidates had been contacted I would repost our adverts weekly and adverts for other sites my colleagues dealt with.

Typically on a Friday I would hold the site tour; with a focus on providing as much information about the site as possible and making sure all candidates acted within health and safety regulations. After giving the site tour I would bring candidates to another area where they undertook health and safety tests, and drug and alcohol tests. I would help in the administration of drug and alcohol tests and would also escort candidates who failed either test off site. After the site tour, tests, and interviews were complete I would get in contact with successful candidates and invite them to our training week the week after.
